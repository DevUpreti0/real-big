Movie Info

Master is a Tamil action-thriller movie, written and directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj of Kaithi fame, and produced by Xavier Britto, under the banner XB Film Creators. The movie stars Vijay and Vijay Sethupathi in the leading roles while Malavika Mohanan, Arjun Das, Andrea Jeremiah and Shanthanu Bhagyaraj play supporting roles.
